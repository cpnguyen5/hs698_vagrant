HS 698 Project

Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) Provider Utilization & Payment Plan

Purpose

Investigate the utilization of services & procedures and the relative healthcare costs amongst providers participating in Medicare and Medicaid programs in order to explore the current quality of care being provided to beneficiaries of the CMS (Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services) fee-for-service programs.

Objectives

  1. Visualize the prevalence of disease/chronic conditions amongst beneficiaries receiving care under Medicare & Medicaid program.
  2. Determine the costs of services and procedures provided to CMS beneficiaries by clinicians by geographic location, facility type, and "provider utilization" (HCPCS codes).
  3. Investigate the relationship between healthcare costs under CMS and demographics (e.g. age, race, gender).
  4. Conduct a case study on beneficiaries identified with cancer (all types) in order to determine the impact of cancer on the CMS programs.
